# Prompt: Personal Portfolio Website for Alex Chan

Create a professional, clean, and modern personal portfolio website for a recent business school graduate. The website should function as a digital resume to attract potential employers.



* **Name:** Alex Chan

* **Professional Title:** Recent Business Graduate | Aspiring Marketing Strategist | Data-Driven Analyst



## 2. Target Audience & Goal



* **Audience:** Potential employers, recruiters, and hiring managers.

* **Goal:** To showcase education, skills, and experience in a compelling way to secure job interviews.



## 3. Tone & Style



* **Tone:** Professional, confident, and approachable.

* **Visual Style:** Minimalist, modern, with ample white space.

* **Color Palette:**

    * **Primary:** Dark Navy Blue (`#0A2342`) and White (`#FFFFFF`).

    * **Accent:** Muted Gold (`#B8860B`) or Calm Teal (`#4682B4`) for buttons, links, and highlights.

* **Typography:** Use a clean, readable sans-serif font family.

    * **Recommended Fonts:** `Lato`, `Montserrat`, or `Poppins`.



## 4. Website Structure (Single-Page Scroll)



### Header/Navigation Bar

* Should be "sticky" (fixed at the top on scroll).

* Include navigation links that scroll to the corresponding sections: `Home`, `About`, `Experience`, `Skills`, `Contact`.



### Section 1: Hero

* **Layout:** Two-column.

* **Left Column:**

    * Professional headshot of Alex Chan (image provided).

* **Right Column:**

    * **Name:** `Alex Chan` (large, prominent font).

    * **Headline:** `Recent Business Graduate from Lingnan University, passionate about leveraging data to drive marketing success.`

    * **Call-to-Action Buttons:**

        1.  `View My Resume` (links to a PDF file).

        2.  `Contact Me` (scrolls to the Contact section).



### Section 2: About Me

* Use the provided "About Me" text.

* Maintain a clean layout with generous spacing.

* (Optional) Use a subtle decorative line or shape to separate this section from the next.



### Section 3: Experience

* **Layout:** A clean, vertical timeline format.

* Use icons to differentiate experience types (e.g., a briefcase icon for internships, a trophy icon for competitions).

* **Content:**

    1.  **Internship Experience:**

        * Display company, role, dates, and a bulleted list of achievements.

    2.  **Case Competition Experience:**

        * Display competition name, role, and a summary of accomplishments.



### Section 4: Education & Skills

* **Layout:** Two-column.

* **Left Column: Education**

    * University Name: `Lingnan University`

    * Degree Title

    * Graduation Date

    * Key Coursework

    * GPA & Honors

* **Right Column: Skills**

    * Organize skills under the following headings:

        * `Languages`

        * `Technical Skills`

        * `Soft Skills`

    * Use a clean list or a tag-cloud format for display.



### Section 5: Contact

* **Heading:** A simple and friendly message like "Let's Connect."

* **Email:** Clearly display the email address.

* **Social Links:** Add clickable icons linking to the LinkedIn profile.

* **(Optional) Contact Form:** A simple form with fields for `Name`, `Email`, and `Message`.



### Footer

* A simple, clean footer containing:

    * `Alex Chan`

    * Email Address

    * Link to LinkedIn Profile



## 5. Technical Requirements



* **Responsiveness:** The website must be fully responsive and optimized for mobile, tablet, and desktop screens.

* **SEO:** Optimize for search engines using keywords such as "Alex Chan," "Lingnan University business graduate," "marketing," and "Hong Kong."
